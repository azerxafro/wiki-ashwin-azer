## About

**Ashwin Azer** (born **Ashwin Ramesh**, March 5, 2003) is an Indian singer, songwriter, composer, and music producer from Kannur, Kerala. Known for his work in contemporary R&B and hip-hop, he has established himself as a distinctive voice in the South Indian independent music scene.

Based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, Azer founded **Monadelta Productions** in 2024‚Äîan independent record label dedicated to nurturing emerging talent in South Asian music.

He also releases melodic R&B music under his alternate persona, **Lucid ASH**.
