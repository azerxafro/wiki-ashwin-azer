# Career

## 2019–2022: Early Career

Ashwin Azer began releasing music independently in 2019, establishing his presence on streaming platforms. During this period, he developed his signature sound and built a following in the South Indian independent music scene.

## 2023: Breakthrough

Following his graduation, Azer increased his musical output and began collaborating with other artists. His work gained traction on streaming platforms, particularly Spotify and Apple Music.

## 2024: Monadelta Productions

In 2024, Azer founded **Monadelta Productions**, an independent record label based in Coimbatore. The label represents his vision for a new wave of South Indian independent music. Co-founded with fellow artist Wa'Cali, Monadelta serves as a platform for emerging artists in the region.

### Label Roster (2024)
- Ashwin Azer (founder)
- Wa'Cali (co-founder)
- Marabukavi
- Saileshxafro

## 2025: DESTINY Album

In 2025, Azer released his debut studio album **DESTINY**, marking a significant milestone in his career. The album showcases his growth as an artist and producer.

## Musical Style

Azer's music is characterized by:
- Melodic rap flows
- R&B vocal techniques
- Atmospheric production
- Introspective lyrics
- Fusion of Western and South Asian influences
