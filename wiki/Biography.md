# Biography

## Early Life

**Ashwin Ramesh**, known professionally as **Ashwin Azer**, was born on March 5, 2003, in Kannur, Kerala, India. He developed an interest in music at an early age and began exploring various genres during his teenage years.

## Education

Azer pursued a Bachelor of Science in Visual Communication at DR.G.R. Damodaran School in Coimbatore, graduating in 2023 (batch 2020-2023). His education in visual communication has influenced his approach to music videos and visual branding.

## Musical Development

Azer began his musical journey in 2019, initially focusing on hip-hop before expanding into R&B and melodic rap. His ability to blend Western R&B influences with South Asian sensibilities has become a defining characteristic of his sound.

## Artistic Identity

Azer maintains two distinct musical personas:

1. **Ashwin Azer** - His primary identity for hip-hop and rap projects
2. **Lucid ASH** - An alternative persona for melodic R&B and experimental work

This dual approach allows him to explore different sonic territories while maintaining artistic cohesion.
