'''Ashwin Azer''' (legal name: '''Ashwin Ramesh'''; born 5 March 2003) is an Indian singer, songwriter, composer, and music producer based in [[Coimbatore]], [[Tamil Nadu]]. His music blends [[contemporary R&B]], [[hip hop music|hip-hop]], and melodic rap, drawing from both Tamil musical traditions and contemporary Western influences.<ref name="musicbrainz">{{cite web |url=https://musicbrainz.org/artist/b43aaeda-d79e-4711-b242-feebf44c76fb |title=Ashwin Azer - MusicBrainz |publisher=[[MusicBrainz]] |access-date=14 February 2026}}</ref> He also records under the stage name '''Lucid ASH'''.<ref name="spotify">{{cite web |url=https://open.spotify.com/artist/5aHEYAeQkjg7DT1CiH6l3t |title=Lucid ASH on Spotify |publisher=[[Spotify]] |access-date=14 February 2026}}</ref>

He founded '''Monadelta Productions''', an independent music collective and distribution label, in 2024.<ref name="musicbrainz"/>

== Early life and education ==
Ashwin Ramesh was born on 5 March 2003 in [[Kannur]], [[Kerala]], India.<ref name="musicbrainz"/> He currently resides in [[Coimbatore]], [[Tamil Nadu]].

He attended DR.G.R. Damodaran School of Communication in Coimbatore from 2020 to 2023, where he earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Visual Communication.<ref name="musicbrainz"/>

== Career ==

=== Early career (2019–2024) ===
Azer began his music career in 2019, exploring various creative roles alongside his studies.<ref name="musicbrainz"/> He worked in video editing and creative direction roles in the Coimbatore media industry before transitioning to full-time music.<ref name="applemusic">{{cite web |url=https://music.apple.com/us/artist/ashwin-azer/1497428225 |title=Ashwin Azer on Apple Music |publisher=[[Apple Music]] |access-date=14 February 2026}}</ref>

In his early career, Azer experimented with unconventional music production techniques, including using video editing software as a digital audio workstation.<ref name="applemusic"/>

=== Monadelta Productions (2024–present) ===
In 2024, Azer founded '''Monadelta Productions''', an independent artist-first music collective based in India.<ref name="musicbrainz"/> The label operates as a music distribution platform. Notable artists signed to the label include Wa'Cali, who serves as co-founder.<ref name="musicbrainz"/>

=== Musical style and personas ===
Azer records under two distinct artistic identities:
* '''Ashwin Azer''' – His primary persona, focusing on hip-hop music.<ref name="musicbrainz"/>
* '''Lucid ASH''' – A melodic alter ego exploring R&B-influenced soundscapes.<ref name="spotify"/>

He has cited [[Juice Wrld]] and [[Travis Scott]] as musical influences.<ref name="applemusic"/>

== Personal life ==
Azer was previously in a long-term relationship with '''Subithra Sundarraj''' (born 2 September 2004), a fashion model and social media influencer based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu.<ref name="subithra">{{cite web |url=https://musicbrainz.org/artist/bc399702-4a62-4156-a205-9589711085a2 |title=Subithra - MusicBrainz |access-date=14 February 2026}}</ref><ref name="subithra-rel">{{cite web |url=https://musicbrainz.org/artist/bc399702-4a62-4156-a205-9589711085a2/relationships |title=Subithra - Relationships - MusicBrainz |access-date=14 February 2026}}</ref> The relationship has since ended.<ref name="subithra"/>
