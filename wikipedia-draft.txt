'''Ashwin Azer''' (legal name: '''Ashwin Ramesh'''; born 5 March 2003) is an Indian singer, songwriter, composer, and music producer based in [[Coimbatore]], [[Tamil Nadu]]. His music blends [[contemporary R&B]], [[hip hop music|hip-hop]], and melodic rap, drawing from both Tamil musical traditions and contemporary Western influences.<ref name="press">{{cite web |url=https://press.ashwinazer.rocks/ |title=Ashwin Azer (Lucid ASH) – Hip-Hop Artist & Producer EPK |access-date=14 February 2026}}</ref><ref name="musicbrainz">{{cite web |url=https://musicbrainz.org/artist/b43aaeda-d79e-4711-b242-feebf44c76fb |title=Ashwin Azer - MusicBrainz |access-date=14 February 2026}}</ref><ref name="medium">{{cite web |url=https://medium.com/@r0b1nmatriz/tamil-r-b-star-on-rise-a5e9211efaeb |title=Tamil R&B Star on Rise |last=De Cruz |first=Robin |date=24 November 2024 |website=[[Medium (website)|Medium]] |access-date=14 February 2026}}</ref> He also records under the stage name '''Lucid ASH'''.<ref name="spotify">{{cite web |url=https://open.spotify.com/artist/5aHEYAeQkjg7DT1CiH6l3t |title=Lucid ASH on Spotify |access-date=14 February 2026}}</ref>

He founded '''Monadelta Productions''', an independent music collective and distribution label, in 2024.<ref name="monadelta">{{cite web |url=https://monadelta.me |title=Monadelta Official Label Portal |access-date=14 February 2026}}</ref>

== Early life and education ==
Ashwin Ramesh was born on 5 March 2003 in [[Kannur]], [[Kerala]], India.<ref name="bio">{{cite web |url=https://ashwinazer.me |title=Ashwin Azer - Personal Bio |access-date=14 February 2026}}</ref> He currently resides in [[Coimbatore]], [[Tamil Nadu]].

He attended DR.G.R. Damodaran School of Communication in Coimbatore from 2020 to 2023, where he earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Visual Communication.<ref name="bio"/>

== Career ==

=== Early career (2019–2024) ===
Azer began his music career in 2019, exploring various creative roles alongside his studies.<ref name="press"/> He worked as an Assistant Director and Art Designer at Film Shades Creation in 2022, later becoming a Senior Video Editor and Creative Manager at The Photo Spot in 2023, and a Content Creator and Video Editor at Sirpi Aesthetics in 2024.<ref name="bio"/>

In his early career, Azer experimented with unconventional music production techniques, including using video editing software as a digital audio workstation.<ref name="applemusic">{{cite web |url=https://music.apple.com/us/artist/ashwin-azer/1497428225 |title=Ashwin Azer on Apple Music |access-date=14 February 2026}}</ref>

=== Monadelta Productions (2024–present) ===
In 2024, Azer founded '''Monadelta Productions''', an independent artist-first music collective based in India.<ref name="monadelta"/> The label operates as a music distribution platform, offering artists unlimited music distribution for ₹369/year. Notable artists signed to the label include Wa'Cali, who serves as co-founder.<ref name="monadelta"/>

=== Musical style and personas ===
Azer records under two distinct artistic identities:
* '''Ashwin Azer''' – His primary persona, focusing on hip-hop music.<ref name="press"/>
* '''Lucid ASH''' – A melodic alter ego exploring R&B-influenced soundscapes.<ref name="spotify"/>

He has cited [[Juice Wrld]] and [[Travis Scott]] as musical influences.<ref name="applemusic"/>

A 2024 article in ''Medium'' described Azer as blending "traditional Tamil sounds with modern R&B and hip-hop influences, creating a unique and refreshing listening experience" and called him "a standout artist in the Tamil music industry."<ref name="medium"/>

== Personal life ==
Azer was previously in a long-term relationship with '''Subithra Sundarraj''' (born 2 September 2004), a fashion model and social media influencer based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu.<ref name="subithra">{{cite web |url=https://musicbrainz.org/artist/bc399702-4a62-4156-a205-9589711085a2 |title=Subithra - MusicBrainz |access-date=14 February 2026}}</ref><ref name="subithra-rel">{{cite web |url=https://musicbrainz.org/artist/bc399702-4a62-4156-a205-9589711085a2/relationships |title=Subithra - Relationships - MusicBrainz |access-date=14 February 2026}}</ref> The relationship has since ended.<ref name="subithra"/>
